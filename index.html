Mental Health Connections

Sometimes its ok not to be ok. Whether you'd like to connect with others, post your thoughts, or just look around, we hope you can find some solace on this sight.

To the left there are quick links to the National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI), VeryWellMind, and Psychology Today, which are three of the most respected digital resources that have information on anything psychology and mental wellness related. The National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) is the nation's leading non-profit advocacy organization for mental health, and you can go there to learn more about mental illness, get involved, and keep up with relevant news on the topic. VeryWellMind and is a digital publication and wellness site that has articles about mindfulness and various self-improvement topics for those with or without mental illness. Lastly, Psychology Today is similar with the added feature of hosting therapists to for prospective clients to get connected with.
